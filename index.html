The Financial Challenge

Professional athletes face a unique set of financial hurdles that traditional advising often overlooks.

Short Career Windows

Average career: 3-5 years. Limited earning windows require rapid wealth accumulation and preservation strategies tailored to high short-term cash flow.

Fragmented Advice

Athletes often work with multiple disconnected advisors (Canada vs USA). Lack of coordination leads to missed opportunities and conflicting advice.
